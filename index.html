Pivotal Cards

This is a fork of another project, but made to suit US page sizes rather than ISO page sizes.

Drag the pivotal-cards bookmarklet to your toolbar, then run the bookmarklet while viewing a Pivotal Tracker page. The page will reformat, suitable for printing. Print the pages in Landscape mode, double-sided, and then cut them up. Easy Peasy!
